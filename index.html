What We Do

AI-based HVAC optimization for commercial and residential buildings.

Smart Energy Management

We monitor real-time HVAC performance using AI to reduce wasted energy and automatically adjust climate settings based on usage patterns.

Predictive Maintenance

Our system detects issues before they happen, preventing sudden failures and reducing repair costs through intelligent fault prediction.

Carbon Footprint Reduction

By optimizing heating, cooling, and ventilation, we help buildings lower emissions and meet sustainability goal without sacrificing comfort.

About

Maple Smart HVAC is a technology-driven HVAC solutions company specializing in AI-powered climate control and system intelligence. We help residential and commercial clients improve energy efficiency, enhance indoor comfort, and extend equipment life through smart monitoring, predictive analytics, and automated optimization. Our approach combines proven HVAC expertise with advanced artificial intelligence to deliver reliable, cost-effective, and future-ready solutions.

  • 2024

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our journey started as a final-year project exploring AI-based energy optimization for HVAC systems. We focused on solving real-world challenges faced by residential and commercial buildings!

  • April 2025

    Supported by Venture Lab

    After presenting our prototype, we were selected and supported by Conestoga Venture Lab. Here, we received mentorship, technical support, and early business guidance that helped transform our idea into a market-ready product!

  • September 2025

    MapleSmart HVAC is Formed

    From a prototype to a startup. We officially formed MapleSmart HVAC with the mission of bringing AI-driven climate control and predictive maintenance into everyday buildings!

  • Now

    Building the Next Generation HVAC

    We’re currently working on real-world deployments, pilot projects, and partnerships to bring smarter, greener, and more affordable HVAC solutions across Canada!
